About Me!

Hi, my name is Daniel David, but sometimes I go by Dexter. I am a young black aspiring full stack developer.

Born and raised in New York City, I've adopted the forward thinking must New Yorkers posses to be great in this melting pot of a city.

I got my introduction to coding in 10th grade, where a company that went by ScriptEd introduced high schoolers to HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

It was there where my love for coding was created.

Making websites with my peers and friends while learning new programming languages was probably the best time I had in high school.

I now code in HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, and Python. I also have intern expereince working at IBM and ETSY.

I love to build in codespaces, and working colaboratively towards a goal is a big motivator in my work.
